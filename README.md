# Measuring AI Economy

Private repo for AI market intelligence aggregation and economic impact measurement.

## Scope (three distinct workstreams)

1. **AI News/Signal Aggregation** — Scoble's AlignedNews + other feeds
2. **Economic Measurement of AI Impact** — BTOS, open source indicators, measurement frameworks
3. **Market Intelligence** — stocks, earnings signals, investment flows, sector analysis
